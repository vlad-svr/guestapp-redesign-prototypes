What's an eSIM?

A SIM card built into your phone. Instead of swapping a plastic chip, you install a profile — the phone then has two numbers: your normal one for calls, and this one for data in Spain.

Need a hand?

Our partner Airalo runs support for everything eSIM. They answer in about 2 hours — support@airalo.com.

When does my eSIM activate?

Automatically, when you arrive in Spain and it connects to a local network. Installing it at home costs you nothing and uses none of your days.

What if I run out of data?

Buy another plan here, or top up the same eSIM from the Airalo app. Your existing eSIM keeps working — the new data is added to it.

Will I keep my normal number?

Yes. The eSIM is a second line for data. Your usual number stays active for calls and messages — keep it set as your default voice line.

Will it work on my phone?
We can't detect your phone from this computer. Three things have to be true:
Supports eSIM. Every iPhone from the XS onwards, and most Android flagships since 2020.
Not carrier-locked. Phones bought outright are unlocked; ask your carrier if you're on a contract.
Not jailbroken or rooted. Modified phones cannot install eSIM profiles.

If your model isn't listed it may be too new, or a regional variant without eSIM support — check with the manufacturer.
