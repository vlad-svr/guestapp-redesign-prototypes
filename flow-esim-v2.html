Frequently asked

When does my eSIM activate?

Automatically, when you arrive in Spain and it connects to a local network. Installing it at home costs you nothing and uses none of your days.

Can I use it straight away?

You can install it any time, but it only starts working when you reach your destination. Install before you fly — airport Wi-Fi is the worst place to do it.

What if I run out of data?

Buy another plan here, or top up the same eSIM from the Airalo app. Your existing eSIM keeps working — the new data is added to it.

Will I keep my normal number?

Yes. The eSIM is a second line for data. Your usual number stays active for calls and messages — keep it set as your default voice line.
