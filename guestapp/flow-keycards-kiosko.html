Key cards — Interactive Flow (Kiosk)

The /remote-keys route is not virtual keys — it drives the physical card dispenser under the kiosk screen. The redesign makes the printing job legible from a metre away: an abstract progress bar becomes a stack of real cards (printed = solid, remaining = dashed ghosts), and every screen that expects the guest to reach for something points at the slot it will come out of. Covers the auto-opening registration reminder, the print → dispense loop, both PrintedCards outcomes, the dispenser failure and the no-room-assigned gate. Mock data only.
