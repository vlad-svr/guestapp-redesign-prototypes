Holds, explained

A hold reserves money on your card without charging it. Here's the whole life of your €300.00 deposit hold:

1When you pay, €300.00 is reserved on your card. It leaves your available balance but is never charged — statements show it as "pending", sometimes as €0.00.
2During your stay nothing happens. The hold just sits there.
3After checkout the hold is released automatically. Banks take 1–7 days to show it back — that timing is the bank's, not the property's.
