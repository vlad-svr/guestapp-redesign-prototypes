Where's my booking reference?

It's a short code (letters + numbers) tied to your reservation. Two reliable places:

Your confirmation email. From the host or platform — labelled "reference", "confirmation code" or "booking ID".
Your booking platform. Open the trip in Airbnb, Booking.com or Vrbo — the confirmation code is on the reservation.
Can't find it? Switch to date & email — no code required.
Contact your host

Ask them to resend your booking link or confirm the reference — then search again.
