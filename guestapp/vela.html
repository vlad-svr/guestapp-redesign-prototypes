Vela Helper — Redesign 2.0 Prototype

Vela is the contextual assistant: a glass rail on desktop, a sparkle FAB opening a right-side glass sheet on mobile. It combines the existing tips engine with new building blocks — quick actions (share link, switch to phone via QR, guest count), verification & registration progress, offers and guidebook shortcuts. No chat — Vela is rule-based tips and shortcuts, not a conversation. Mock data only.
