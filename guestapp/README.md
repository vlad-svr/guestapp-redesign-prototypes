# Redesign 2.0 — UX Prototypes

Static HTML/CSS mockups (no build, no app logic, mock data) of the redesigned guest flows with proposed UX improvements layered on top of the existing `@chekinapp/tokens` design system.

## How to view

Open `index.html` in a browser — it links to all prototypes and documents every proposed change with its rationale.

**Proposal vs current.** Where a feature has two prototypes, the one marked **Proposal** is a redesign
proposal shown *beside* the **current** shipped variant — it is not the main variant, and nothing is
decided until it is chosen. The floating nav and `index.html` badge them the same way.

One canonical prototype per feature and form factor — organized by feature, with 📱 mobile and 🖥 desktop grouped together. Most are click-through flows; two are static screen sets (Home, desktop registration). `index.html` and the floating nav on every page follow the same feature-first grouping.

| `flow-splash-v2.html` | Splash screen · 📱 | A rebuild of `components/SplashScreen`, clocked. The shipped component holds every guest for **`load + 5000 ms`** (the timer starts *after* the data arrives), closes two curtains over its own logo at **4.3 s** — leaving an opaque brand-coloured rectangle with no logo, loader or text until dismissal — and, when auth-init hangs, **never unmounts**, because that 5 s timer is its only timer and it is gated behind the data having already landed. Here the splash is a **handoff, not a gate**: it is `position:absolute` over a **shape-matched skeleton** of the screen it becomes, held for **`max(load, 500 ms)`**, and on ready the surface fades while the mark **travels to the header logo slot**. A four-rung timing contract replaces the single constant — **silent below 1.2 s**, "taking longer than usual" + elapsed clock + *Try again* at **6 s**, a **watchdog** at **20 s** that renders a real error state (*Try again* · *Find my booking* · *Message your host* · copyable reference). The loader stays the three pulsing dots, which are **indeterminate by construction** — there is no percentage here to get wrong — but they are drawn as real elements rather than the shipped `box-shadow: 9999px` trick, which assistive tech cannot see at all. Adds the **offline**, **host-branded** and **splash-disabled** states the component has never had; the host logo cross-fades inside a **reserved 96 px box** instead of reflowing, and `--sp-ink` is derived from the host background's luminance so navy-on-navy is impossible. Seven states. The **surface stays V1's** — flat brand ground, bare logo, the three pulsing dots, and the two **curtains** — but the curtains fire on **data-ready** rather than on mount, closing over a screen that is already rendered and clearing 340 ms later, so the motion survives and the dead rectangle does not. Every change here is behavioural, not decorative |

## Ground rules used

- **No new colors** — every value comes from `packages/tokens` in `../chekin-ui` (brand `#385bf8`, navy `#161643`, cool lavender neutrals, Poppins).
- **Liquid glass is chrome-only** — `backdrop-filter` on top bars, bottom nav, on-camera pills and CTA docks; never on dense reading surfaces. Solid `rgba` fallbacks included.
- **One glass version — Minimal, baked in.** The former Full · Minimal / Standard · Liquid-glass A/B switches were removed to match the redesigned app: the pre-auth pages (Find booking, Error, Sign-in/Onboarding, Housing IV) hard-set `html.glass-lite`, and the strong/frosted variants (`glass-*-toggle` scripts, `html.glass-strong` / `html.glass-max` overrides) are gone. Boxes render flat — `--shadow-card` / `--shadow-elev` are no-op tokens; only the focus ring, the brand CTA glow and the baseline chrome glass remain.
- **Motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.**
- Poppins is loaded from Google Fonts for the prototype only; the app self-hosts the same family.
