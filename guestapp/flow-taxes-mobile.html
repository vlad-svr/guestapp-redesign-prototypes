About the tourist tax

Quick answers to the three questions everyone asks:

It's the law, not a property fee. The region requires Villa Serena to collect it and pass it to the local tourism board. It's never part of the room rate.
The math is fixed by the season. €3.10 per adult per night here, and only the first 5 nights are taxed — longer stays don't keep paying.
Exemptions need proof. Minors, residents and other exempt guests are verified at check-in — bring the document that proves it.
View privacy policy
Who can be exempt here

Set by the region, not the property. Every exemption is verified at check-in — the right document makes it stick.

Under 18 — verified by ID or passport.
Registered resident — residence certificate.
Disability of 33% or more — disability certificate.
Subsidized program stay — program letter.
Remove this exemption?

The guest goes back to paying the full rate and the total updates right away. You can re-add the exemption any time before you pay.
