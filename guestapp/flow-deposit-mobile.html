The €300.00 deposit, step by step

Nothing is charged. A pre-authorisation asks your bank to set money aside; it never reaches the host unless damage is reported. Which of the two variants below applies is set per property by is_pre_auth_active.

Retention this property
When you pay €300.00 is temporarily blocked on your card. It is not charged.
Every 7 days the block is released and automatically renewed — so your bank may send you a renewal notice more than once.
It stops renewing after 30 days, or 7 days after check-out — whichever comes first.
Then the amount is released in full and available again.
Pre-authorisation other properties
Nothing is set aside. No funds are blocked and your card limit is untouched.
You authorise the property to store the card and make one charge later, if there is damage.
7 days after check-out that authorisation expires on its own.

If damage occurs

  • The host has 7 days after check-out to review the property.
  • Within that window they may charge the deposit, in part or in full.
  • After it, the money is yours regardless.

Premium protection by Stellar

A one-time, non-refundable fee that replaces the deposit. Accidental damage up to €1,500 is covered by the insurer, not by you.

Covered

  • Accidental damage to the property and its contents
  • Instant from check-in — no deposit blocked on your card

Not covered

  • Deliberate damage, theft, or anything above €1,500
  • The €39.00 is not refunded if you cancel or nothing breaks

Damage waiver — €1,500 plan

1. What this plan is

This damage waiver is not insurance. It transfers responsibility for accidental damage to the accommodation, up to the stated limit, from the guest to the plan provider.

2. Limit of liability

The maximum payable under this waiver is €1,500 per reservation, in aggregate, regardless of the number of incidents.

3. Exclusions

Intentional acts, gross negligence, theft, damage caused by pets where pets are not permitted, and any loss reported more than 14 days after check-out.

4. Refunds

The plan fee is earned in full at purchase and is non-refundable, including where the reservation is cancelled.
