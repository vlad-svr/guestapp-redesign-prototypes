How the €300.00 hold works

A hold is not a payment. Your bank reserves the amount and releases it — you're never charged unless the property reports damage.

It needs available balance. The €300.00 has to be free on the card when you pay, even though it isn't taken.
It shows as "pending". Some banks display it as a €0.00 check instead. It disappears after checkout.
There's an alternative. Damage protection is €39.00 charged once, with nothing reserved.
