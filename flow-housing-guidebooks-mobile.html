Getting around Málaga

Everything below assumes you're arriving at Málaga–Costa del Sol airport (AGP) and heading for the villa.

From the airport to Marbella

  • The direct bus (line M-220) leaves every 90 minutes from terminal T3 — about 50 minutes. Tickets from the driver, cash only.
  • Taxis are a fixed €75 to Marbella old town.
  • Ride-hailing works at the airport but not in the town centre.

Parking near the villa

Blue lines are paid 09:00–20:00. The underground car park on Calle Ancha is €18/day and two minutes' walk away.
