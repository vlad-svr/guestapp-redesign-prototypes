Getting Around Málaga

Málaga is compact and very walkable, but here's how to reach everything else from Villa Serena.

From the airport (AGP)

The C1 Cercanías train runs to María Zambrano station every 20 minutes (€2.30, 12 min). Taxis are a flat €25 to the centre. Uber and Cabify both work here.

Buses & metro

  • Single bus ticket €1.40, buy from the driver.
  • The metro (lines 1 & 2) is handy for the university and stadium.
  • Download the EMT Málaga app for live times.

Parking

Street parking is blue-zone (paid 9:00–20:00, free Sundays). The nearest car park is SMASSA Calle Larios, two minutes from the villa.

Bikes & scooters

The seafront cycle path is flat and gorgeous. Málagabici docks are dotted along the promenade.
