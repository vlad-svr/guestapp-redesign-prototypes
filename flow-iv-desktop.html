How we treat your photos
Identity photos are required by local law — here is exactly what happens to them.
Encrypted end to end. In transit and at rest — nobody at the property sees the raw images.
Used only for this verification. Matching your face to your document, nothing else.
Deleted after your stay. Removed automatically per GDPR retention rules.
